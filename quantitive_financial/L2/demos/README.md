## Makefiles for Monte-Carlo Engines
### Step 1 :
            source env_sdx.sh

### Step 2 :
            make run_sw_emu  or  make run_hw_emu
Waiting for the build of xclbin, and once it's finished, the sw or hw xclbin will be put in xclbin dir
Done!
